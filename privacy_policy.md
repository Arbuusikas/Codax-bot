# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 11, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains what data the Codax Discord Bot ("the Bot") collects, how it is used, and how it is stored. By interacting with the Bot, you acknowledge this policy.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

When you interact with the Bot, the following data may be collected:

| Data | When Collected |
|------|----------------|
| Discord User ID | When you open a ticket or receive a license key |
| Discord Username | When you open a ticket or receive a license key |
| License key issued | When a key is generated and delivered to you |
| Staff member ID and username | Logged at the time a key is issued |
| Discord Message ID | Logged when the key delivery DM is sent |
| Message content (in ticket channels) | Read to facilitate support; not stored by the Bot |
| Guild member data | Used to match ticket channels to users on bot restart |

---

## 2. Why We Collect This Data

- **Ticket system**: Your Discord ID and username are used to create and manage a private purchase ticket channel on your behalf.
- **Key delivery logs**: When a license key is issued to you, your Discord ID, username, the issuing staff member's details, and the key itself are sent to our backend server for record-keeping and fraud prevention.
- **Role assignment**: After a key is issued, your Discord ID is used to assign you the appropriate role in the server.
- **Guild member intent**: Member data is accessed solely to restore in-memory ticket state when the Bot restarts; it is not stored persistently.
- **Message content intent**: The Bot reads messages in ticket channels only to operate the ticket workflow (e.g., detecting activity). Message content is not logged or stored by the Bot.

---

## 3. Data Storage

- Ticket state is held **in memory only** and is cleared when the Bot restarts.
- Key delivery records (Discord ID, username, key, sender info, message ID) are stored on our **backend server** and are used for support and fraud prevention purposes.
- No message content from your ticket channel is stored on our servers.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. Data is only transmitted between the Bot and our own private backend API over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection.

---

## 5. Data Retention

Key delivery logs are retained as long as necessary to resolve disputes, provide support, and prevent fraud. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us via a ticket in the Discord server; we will honour requests where legally permitted and technically feasible.

---

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
- Request correction or deletion of your data.
- Object to or restrict processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, open a ticket in our Discord server.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from users under the age of 13. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version in this document. Continued use of the Bot after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

---

## 9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, open a ticket in our Discord server.
